Basic Info

The blue big Yoshi is a Baby Yoshi that is only found in the Star World, where they hatch from eggs of the same colour whenever Mario makes his approach. If you feed the Baby Yoshies - such as enemies, shells, coins, or active grab blocks, it will turn the yoshi into an adult Yoshi

Things you didn't know about Yoshi

The Yoshi is a very popular character in the Nintendo world, so here are some cool facts
Yoshi's real name is actually T.Yoshisaur Munchakoopas
The character istelf is believed to have been influenced by the game 'Devil World'
Yoshi has a long tongue that can be used to eat anything
Yoshi can beat enemies by also laying eggs and throwing them
Yoshi comes in various different colours, and sometimes they even have special skills
Yoshi has a girlfriend named Birdo!
